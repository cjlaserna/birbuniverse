"What is Pengi Game?"

"To sum it up as simple as possible, Pengi Game is a funky platformer Shooter similar to metal slug and contra about 4 birbs saving the world as they beat the sh*t out of everything."

"Who are the developers of this game?"

"This is a one/two person project. I (Tonatiuh) do all the art and code, but my boy Negativity helps with code. Music/sound design is still a wonky subject."
